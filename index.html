Meet Janika Van Tonder

"The Tragedy in Redwood Valley" marks Janika's debut as a novelist, but her passion for storytelling and mysteries has been a lifelong journey.

Drawing inspiration from classic detective fiction and modern psychological thrillers, Janika crafts stories that explore the complexities of human nature and the secrets we keep from those closest to us.

When she's not writing, Janika enjoys reading and spending time with friends and family, and is already working on Detective Sarah Anderson's next case.

This is just the beginning of what promises to be an exciting literary career.
