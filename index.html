Meet the Characters

Step into Redwood Valley and discover the people whose lives will be forever changed by one tragic night.

Detective Sarah Anderson

Age 23 • Ambitious • Determined

Fresh from the police academy with a keen eye for details and an unshakeable work ethic. Sarah dreams of following in her detective parents' footsteps, but when veteran Detective Ryan gets the lead role she wanted, she must prove herself worthy of the badge.

Her greatest strength: Never gives up on finding the truth

Her weakness: Too hard on herself when things go wrong

Detective Ryan

Age 29 • Experienced • Haunted by the Past

A charming detective from the city, Ryan came to Redwood Valley to start over after a case went terribly wrong. His experience and tenacity make him the perfect partner for Sarah... if she can learn to trust him.

His strength: Knows when to push and when to step back

His struggle: Sometimes drowns his demons in a bottle

Mrs. Brown (The Victim)

Beloved Botanist • Community Heart

Everyone in Redwood Valley loved Mrs. Brown. She was the botanist who knew every plant in the valley, the neighbor who brought soup when you were sick. Her brutal murder in the woods shocks the entire community.

The mystery: Who could want to hurt someone so beloved?

Sarah's connection: Mrs. Brown helped raise her - this case is personal

The Killer

Hidden in Plain Sight

In a town where everyone knows everyone, the murderer has been hiding among them all along. Respected, trusted, even beloved by some... but carrying a deadly secret that Mrs. Brown accidentally discovered.

The twist: Sometimes the most dangerous people are the ones you'd never suspect
